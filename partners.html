CO-ORGANISERS

Iteca Kazakhstan

Iteca Kazakhstan Exhibition Company has been operating in the market of Kazakhstan for more than 25 years and annually holds about 30 exhibitions and conferences of international level.

Exhibitions, organized by the company, are held in Almaty, Atyrau, Nur-Sultan, Shymkent and gather the business audience of the entire Central Asian region.Thematic events of the company cover 22 different sectors of the economy, including: oil and gas, energy, mining and metallurgy, construction, tourism, transport and logistics, food processing and agriculture, medicine.

Iteca Uzbekistan

Iteca Exhibitions - organiser of international exhibitions and conferences in Uzbekistan on strategic areas of the economy, including construction, the food industry, healthcare, oil & gas, transport, the textile industry, energy, chemicals, machinery and other sectors.

The debut exhibition in Uzbekistan had place in 1995. The company has determined efforts during its activity in Uzbekistan have made it possible to attract to Uzbekistan new companies that first learned about the country's economic potential through participation in niche-specialist projects of Iteca Exhibitions.
